The options for \( K\to \pi^{+} \pi^{-} \) reconstruction.

Enumerator
K_MASS 

The vertex and tracks fulfilling the best kaon mass constraint are taken.

KSKL 

The vertex closest to the IP and fulfilling the best kaon mass is taken as \( K_S \), then another one is taken as \( K_L \).

CLOSEST 

The vertex closest to the IP is taken (no kaon mass condition).

BOOST 

Reconstruction using Lorentz boost method.
